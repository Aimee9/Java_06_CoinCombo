Coin Combinations App lets a user enter an amount of cents and shows the smallest number of quarters, dimes, nickels, and pennies needed to make that change

1.Input:1C
Output:1penny

2.Input:5C
Output:1Nickel

3.Input:6C
Output:1Nickel,1Penny

4.Input:10C
Output:1dime

5.Input:16C
Output:1Dime,1Nickel,1Penny

6.Input:25C
Output:1Quarter

7.Input:41C
Output:1Quarter,1Dime,1Nickel,1Penny
